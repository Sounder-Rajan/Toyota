The Toyota Supra (Japanese: トヨタ・スープラ, Hepburn: Toyota Sūpura)
is a sports car and grand tourer manufactured by theToyota Motor
Corporation beginning in 1978. The name "supra"is derived from
the Latin prefix,meaning "above", "to surpass" or "go beyond".
